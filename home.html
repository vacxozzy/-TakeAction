#BlackLivesMatter

Black Lives Matter is a activist movement that was originally made by the african-american community that goes beyond extrajudicial killings of Black people by police and vigilantes. Its purpose is to stop the violence against the Black community that is led by racism. #BlackLivesMatter is working for a world where Black lives are no longer systematically and intentionally targeted for demise.
